SKILLS

User Research

I employ a blend of qualitative and quantitative methods to understand users' needs and behaviors deeply. I use tools like surveys, interviews, and usability testing to gather valuable insights. These insights guide my design decisions, ensuring that the final product resonates with and addresses the users' preferences and pain points effectively.

Interaction Design

Leveraging a blend of empathy and creativity, I craft intuitive user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes that transform complex concepts into intuitive interactions. Figma is my trusted companion in this endeavor, allowing me to iterate rapidly and refine designs with precision.

Visual Design

I shape compelling digital experiences using Figma alongside ADA accessibility tools to craft inclusive and visually appealing digital experiences. From sleek interfaces to captivating graphics, I ensure every element contributes to a cohesive user experience with thoughtful typography, color schemes, and imagery.

Prototyping

Using tools like Figma, I convert ideas into interactive experiences. These prototypes facilitate thorough testing and iterative refinement, allowing stakeholders to visualize the final product and gather user feedback effectively.
